About

Disinformation Hub is a platform designed to empower students and educators to tackle the challenges of identifying and understanding mis/disinformation. Built by students at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, our mission is to foster critical thinking and promote media literacy in an increasingly digital world.

For teachers, we provide a powerful tool to complement your curriculum, offering engaging challenges and informational content that help students differentiate between real and fake information online. With performance insights, such as a point system build into our interactive AI-generated content identification game, we support you in tailoring your teaching strategies.

Our work is inspired by the global urgency to combat disinformation, exemplified in this compelling CBS 60 Minutes segment. Finland, a country on the front lines of the Ukraine war's misinformation battle, equips students with the skills to identify fake news.

Join us in preparing the next generation to become savvy, responsible digital citizens.
